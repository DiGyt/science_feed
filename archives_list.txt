Just an unstructured list of all (?) archives used across science:

📌 Multidisciplinary & Large Aggregator Preprint Platforms

These servers cover many fields across science, engineering, social sciences, and humanities.

arXiv – broad scientific and quantitative fields (physics, math, CS, quantitative bio, stats, econ, etc.)

OSF Preprints – umbrella platform aggregating many subject-specific archives

Preprints.org – multidisciplinary open access archive

Research Square – broad science and medicine preprints

SSRN (Social Science Research Network) – general preprints across many fields including sciences

Figshare – multidisciplinary repository often used for preprints and research outputs

Dryad – open data and often linked preprints/data (less traditional server)

Pangea – repository with preprint and research outputs across disciplines

🔬 Science, Engineering & Technology-Oriented Preprint Servers
Natural & Physical Sciences

bioRxiv – life sciences and biology

medRxiv – clinical and health sciences

ChemRxiv – chemistry preprints

viXra – general e-prints across sciences (less moderated)

CERN Preprints (CDS) – particle physics and related HEP content

OSF Community & Subject-Focused Servers

(Many originate from the OSF Preprints network; discipline varies from science to social science)

agriRxiv – agriculture & allied sciences

EarthArXiv – earth & planetary sciences

EcoEvoRxiv – ecology, evolution & conservation

ECSarXiv – electrochemistry & solid state sciences

engrXiv – engineering & technology

BioHackrXiv – bio-informatics/biological community hacks/events

MarXiv – marine & ocean sciences

PaleorXiv – paleontology

NutriXiv – nutritional science

MindRxiv – mind, contemplative studies

📊 Social Sciences, Humanities, & Cross-Field Archives

Many of these also accept scholarship that overlaps scientific and applied research.

SocArXiv – social sciences

PsyArXiv – psychology & behavioral sciences

CrimRxiv – criminology, justice

Arabixiv – Arabic language multidisciplinary service

AfricArXiv – Africa-focused multidisciplinary archive

BodoArXiv – medieval and historical studies

LawArXiv – legal scholarship

EdArXiv – education science

MetaArXiv – openness/transparency in research

🌍 Regional & Specialized Preprint Servers

ChinaXiv – multidisciplinary Chinese archive

Preprints.ru – Russian multidisciplinary archive

ARPHA Preprints – linked to Pensoft journals, covers many sciences

AAS Open Research – Africa-based multidisciplinary, science & medicine

AMRC Open Research – medical sciences via UK charities

PhilSci-Archive – philosophy of science (overlaps with history/philosophy of science)

PhilArchive – philosophy (broad)

Authorea – collaborative writing platform with preprint hosting (multidisciplinary)

📂 Other Platforms That Include Preprint Content

(These are not always classic “preprint servers” but host prepublication work or early research outputs)

Institutional Repositories (universities & research centers) – many host preprints even if not labeled that way

National repositories (e.g., HAL in France) – host preprints among research outputs

Semantic Scholar / OpenAlex – indexes preprints from many servers, not a server itself

🧠 Notes on Scope & Coverage

Not all platforms restrict by discipline — some are fully multidisciplinary (e.g., arXiv, Preprints.org, Research Square).

OSF Preprints acts as a network hub with many specialized servers, and the list continues to grow.

This list excludes institutional working-paper series that are discipline-specific but not typical preprint servers (e.g., NBER or RePEc working papers), unless they function as open preprint archives.
